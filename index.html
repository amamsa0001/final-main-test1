Did you Know?

Globally, we produce an astounding 40 million tons of electronic waste each year, a staggering equivalent of discarding 800 laptops every single second. Furthermore, the average cellphone user replaces their device approximately every 18 months, contributing significantly to this escalating crisis, electronics make up 70% of our overall toxic waste.

Recycling Process

Here's a step-by-step process that is easy to remember:

  • Collect Electronics
  • Transport Electronics to a Certified Electronic Recycler
  • Shred Electronics
  • Sort Electronics
  • Dust Extraction
  • Magnetic Separation
  • Water Separation
  • Purification
  • Prep for resale
